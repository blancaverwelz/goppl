What you need to know about Go People?

Dispatch Today, Deliver Today!

27 Aug, 2020 By Manuel Blancaver

Share

During these uncertain times, whether you are trying to send a business parcel or waiting for a personal package it is important to know the status of it. Picture a day when you actually need to receive a package but you’re clueless whether it will arrive that same day or it might be delayed in a couple of days. Well worry not! Go People offer the smartest same-day delivery solution.

Go People has the only tracking system that offers both senders and receivers a live GPS tracking, showing you from the pick-up status to drop-off and a live update of the location of the Runner. It is the perfect solution to track your packages!

In our day-to-day business, work or personal life, time is very important and managing your daily activities and tasks can be mind-boggling at times. With that, delivering or waiting to receive a package would probably be your least priority or maybe at the top of your list. That’s why Go People made it more flexible for you to have it delivered on demand as soon as possible or have it delivered on a certain book-by times. (Learn more here)

With the increased efficiency of our existing courier services, Go People still continues to improve and deliver for you and your customers with a high reliability rating and professionalism.

We want to make sure that we provide first class experience to both senders and recipients. As CEO of Go People, Wayne Wang quoted “Go People will reshape the delivery world”.
